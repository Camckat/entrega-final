Meet the Characters

The Kids

They are the main characters of the story and who, after the disappearance of one of them in strange circumstances, begin to realize that something very strange happens in this town in a supernatural way and not very different from their beloved game Dungeons and Dragons.

The Teenagers

Although they are not the main characters in the series, without them it would not have been possible to uncover the mystery. They are a great support for the children and together they make a great team.

The Adults

The adults in this story do their best to solve this mystery and use their knowledge to find Will and protect others from falling into the upside down.
